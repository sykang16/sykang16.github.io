Introduction

The Inventory of Owned and Leased Properties (IOLP) dataset contains information about properties owned or leased by the General Services Administration (GSA). These properties are located across the United States, Puerto Rico, Guam, and American Samoa. The dataset helps people understand how government buildings and spaces are used, whether they are owned or rented, and provides details about their location, size, and condition.

Interactive Map

Interactive Map of GSA-Owned and Leased Properties

The interactive map visualizes GSA-owned and leased properties across the United States. Each point represents a property, and the colors distinguish properties by state. You can hover over points to see detailed information about each property, including: ● The property's name ● The city and state ● The building's rentable square feet ● The available square feet ● The lease expiration date Key insights include the widespread distribution of properties across all states, with clusters in major cities and states with higher activity. This map allows users to explore the details of each property and understand its geographic distribution interactively.
